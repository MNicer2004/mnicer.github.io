One Piece

Episodes: 1,061 +

Release Date: October 20, 1999

One Piece is a long-running anime series about a group of pirates led by Monkey D. Luffy as they search for the ultimate treasure known as the One Piece. Along the way, they encounter numerous enemies and make new allies, while uncovering the secrets of the world they live in. The series is known for its humor, action-packed battles, and emotional storytelling.

Attack on Titan

Episodes: 88 +

Release Date: April 7, 2013

"Attack on Titan" is a popular anime series set in a dystopian world where humanity lives in fear of Titans, humanoid creatures who eat humans without reason. The story follows Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert, who join the military to fight against Titans after their hometown is destroyed. As they progress in their fight, they uncover dark secrets about their world and the true nature of Titans. The anime's themes explore the concepts of survival, betrayal, and the human condition in times of crisis.

Watch Now

Demons Slayer

Episodes: 60 +

Release Date: April 6, 2019

"Demon Slayer" is an anime series that follows the story of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy whose family is killed by demons, and his journey to become a demon slayer in order to avenge his family and protect others. Along with his sister, Nezuko, who has been turned into a demon, Tanjiro trains under the guidance of a skilled swordsman and together they travel to battle powerful demons and unravel the mysteries of their existence. The series features intense action sequences, stunning animation, and a compelling story that explores themes of grief, perseverance, and the bonds of family.

Watch Now

My Hero Academia

Episodes: 113

Release Date: April 3, 2016

My Hero Academia is a popular Japanese anime series that follows the story of a young boy named Izuku Midoriya who dreams of becoming a hero despite being born without any superpowers, known as "Quirks". After being chosen by the world's greatest hero, All Might, to inherit his Quirk, Izuku enrolls in U.A. High School, a prestigious academy for aspiring heroes. Along with his classmates, Izuku faces various challenges and battles villains in order to become the next symbol of peace and justice. The anime is known for its well-developed characters, emotional storylines, and action-packed fight scenes.

Watch Now

Blue Lock

Episodes: 24

Release Date: March 25, 2023

"Blue Lock" is a sports anime that revolves around a group of talented soccer players who are brought together to participate in a unique training program. The program aims to find and cultivate the world's greatest striker, and the players are pitted against each other in a fierce competition to see who will emerge as the ultimate striker. Along the way, the players face personal challenges and rivalries, as they strive to achieve their individual and collective goals. The series features intense soccer matches, strategic gameplay, and complex character relationships.

Watch Now

Jujutsu Kaisen

Episodes: 24

Release Date: October 3, 2020

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular anime series that follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes a Jujutsu Sorcerer after swallowing a cursed object. Along with his fellow sorcerers, including the stern but caring Gojo Satoru and the tough-talking Nobara Kugisaki, Yuji fights against powerful curses that threaten the safety of humanity. The series is known for its intense action scenes, unique and diverse characters, and engaging plot that explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the consequences of power.
